Simon Says the Weather

Problem

How might we inform busy adults about the weather so they can plan their day?

The weather affects everyone, from what we wear to our daily schedule. It can be quite unpredictable, especially in my area. If we are not prepared, then our whole day can be thrown off. The goal of this app to make sure busy adults, those who may not always be thinking about the weather, are informed and ready to act.

Solution

Meet Simon, a personal assistant to help you with the weather

Similar to voice assistants, like Amazon Alexa, Google Home, and Siri, Simon is a user's personal weatherman. He works as hard as the busy people he informs about the weather.

Widgets prepare you for the weather outside the app

Instead of bringing people to the forecast, let's bring the forecast to the people. Widgets allow Simon to report the weather on the platforms busy adults use.

Notifications to report the weather when you need it

Research showed that people check the weather the most during the morning. Notifications can report the weather in the morning while also alerting busy adults to weather changes throughout the day.

Research

User Interviews and Analysis

My first step in the process was to talk to my potential users: busy adults. In conducted 5 interviews with individuals in my area to understand how often they check the weather and how it affects their day.

Competitive Analysis

Some of the users I interviewed already used a weather app. I investigated the following applications to understand their experiences

Busy adults already use many digital applications for work and play

Maybe we could design a way to provide the forecast in apps busy adults already use

Users spend a lot of time thinking about how to dress for the weather

Maybe we could make suggestions on how busy adults could prepare what they wear

Weather apps vary in features and in how they visualize the forecast

Maybe we could think of a new way to report the weather and adapt existing heuristics
